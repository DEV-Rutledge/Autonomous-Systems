## Scorecard

| Metric | Score (1-10) | Reasoning |
|---|---|---|
| Market Demand | 8 | Real documented case studies at $10–50K/mo for lean, specialized agencies; clear existing willingness to pay for both project work and retainers |
| Automation Potential | 7 | The deliverable is automation, but the sales/delivery motion itself is bespoke per engagement — docked relative to a pure SaaS/content play |

| Time to MVP | 9 | No product-build phase; first portfolio piece achievable in 1–2 weeks within a 10–20 hrs/week schedule |
| Scalability | 7 | Bespoke work caps pure scalability vs. SaaS, but productizing a niche offer plus an AI-agent premium tier improves this over time |
| Personal Interest | 9 | Explicit top pick in direct interview, independent of revenue math |
