# Idea Validation Checklist

**Stage 2 of the [Incubator Pipeline](README.md#pipeline)** — a fast-fail gate applied right after an idea is captured, before investing time in full market research. If most of these aren't checkable with a straight face, don't proceed to [MarketResearchTemplate.md](MarketResearchTemplate.md) yet.

## Checklist

- [ ] Problem is painful
- [ ] Customers already spend money on solving it
- [ ] Recurring revenue is possible
- [ ] Automation potential is >80%
- [ ] Can reach a first customer in <60 days
- [ ] API-first architecture is feasible
- [ ] AI provides a durable advantage (not just a thin wrapper)
- [ ] Clear acquisition channel exists
- [ ] Expansion opportunities are identifiable

Copy this list into `Ideas/<idea-slug>/ValidationChecklist.md` and check off items per idea — see [README.md § How to Use This Folder](README.md#how-to-use-this-folder).
